Photonics

WDM & Signal Multiplexing

Wavelength Division Multiplexing allows multiple data streams to travel simultaneously on a single fibre, massively boosting throughput without new physical infrastructure.

Wireless

Li-Fi in Indoor Networks

By modulating LED lights faster than the human eye can detect, Li-Fi achieves multi-Gbps links indoors — ideal for hospitals, aircraft, and high-security environments.

AI/ML

Edge Intelligence

Running inference models directly on IoT devices reduces latency and cloud dependency, enabling real-time decisions in smart homes, robotics, and wearables.

Distributed sensor meshes using MQTT and LoRaWAN allow city-scale environmental monitoring, predictive maintenance, and precision agriculture at low cost.

Quantum Cryptography

Quantum key distribution (QKD) uses the laws of quantum mechanics to create theoretically unbreakable encryption, revolutionizing secure communications.

Blockchain

Smart Contracts & DeFi

Self-executing contracts on decentralized networks eliminate the need for intermediaries in finance, supply chains, and digital ownership systems.
